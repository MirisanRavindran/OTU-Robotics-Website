Unleashing Innovation in Robotics

Welcome to one of the largest student based projects/clubs, we consist of two teams of Ri3D and RoboMaster. We showcase our commitment to display our team's diverse talent and pushing the boundaries of robotics innovation through these twop teams. In the heart of Ontario Tech's Automotive Centre of Excellence, Ri3D brings together over 100 dedicated students annually to design, build, and program robots within a tight three-day window, addressing the challenges posed by the FIRST Robotics Competition. Our live-streamed build season and mentorship programs contribute to a collaborative community and offer valuable insights into diverse design concepts. On the global stage, RoboMaster University Series (RMUL), founded by DJI, stands as a beacon of excellence in robotic competitions and academic exchange. Since its inception in 2013, RoboMaster has empowered over 50,000 young learners from 400+ colleges and universities worldwide. Through intense team battles and strategic planning, participants gain industry experience, bridging textbook knowledge with practical skills. Join us on this transformative journey, where both Ri3D and RoboMaster exemplify our dedication to fostering the next generation of engineering talents and technological visionaries.
