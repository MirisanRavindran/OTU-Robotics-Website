RoboMaster University Series: Shaping Future Innovators

Dive into the dynamic world of RoboMaster University Series (RMUL), a pioneering platform founded by DJI that transcends conventional boundaries in the realm of robotics and academic exchange. Established in 2013, RMUL is not just a competition; it's a transformative journey for global technology enthusiasts. Our mission is clear - to empower young learners to reshape their world with the formidable power of engineering and technology. At RMUL, we challenge participants to move beyond textbooks, encouraging the formation of robotics teams that delve into the creation of a diverse fleet of robots. Engaging in intense team battles, students gain invaluable industry experience and cultivate strategic planning skills. With over 400 colleges and universities from around the globe participating annually, RMUL has become a hub for fostering the talents of over 50,000 young learners. More than a competition, RoboMaster is a testament to the endless possibilities of technological innovation. Join us in this exciting journey where book smarts meet practical skills, creating the most advanced and intelligent robots that showcase the potential of the future. RoboMaster isn't just a challenge; it's a celebration of creativity, collaboration, and the pursuit of engineering excellence.

Our Subteams

These subteams are what make up our Robotic teams.

Mechanical Team

Crafting the physical essence of our robots, the Mechanical Team designs with precision and innovation, ensuring robust structures and intricate mechanisms.

Programming Team

Breathing life into our robots, the Programming Team employs expert coding to enhance agility and intelligence, bringing seamless execution of strategies during competitions.

Computer Vision Team

Pioneering perception capabilities, the Computer Vision Team utilizes cutting-edge technology, including motion tracking and machine learning, to track opponents' armor plates with unparalleled accuracy.

Electrical Team

The power behind our robots, the Electrical Team ensures optimal performance by seamlessly integrating electronics, handling everything from wiring to power distribution.

Business Team

Driving project success behind the scenes, the Business Team manages operations, leads fundraising efforts, and fosters crucial connections with the community.
